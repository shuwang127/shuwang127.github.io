Professional Profile

Shu Wang is a Ph.D. student in the Center for Secure Information Systems (CSIS) at George Mason University, under the supervision of Dr. Kun Sun. Before that, he obtained his bachelor's degree in Communication Engineering in 2014 and master's degree in Signal and Information Processing in 2017 from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications. He also works as a graduate research assistant at the Center for Secure Information Systems (CSIS). His research interests lie primarily in the fields of information security and machine learning.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in security research and machine learning. My current interests mainly focus on the following domains:

  • Software Security
  • Graph Neural Network
  • Adversarial Machine Learning
  • Signal Processing
